Projects Content

The Halo Chain began with the simple idea to deliver Halloween-themed goodie bags to the patients at Dell Children’s Center in October of 2021. Originally, the goal was to delivery twenty goodie bags with a fundraising goal of $100. By the end of the week, over $1,000 were donated and we were able to put together 360 goodie bags — enough for every single child at the hospital!

In January of 2021, we worked with the Texas Sweethearts, a spirit organization at the University of Texas at Austin, to put together Valentine’s Day themed activity books for a delivery on February 14th. Each child is set to receive a folder with coloring pages, puzzles, stickers, and colored pencils. We can’t wait to make this delivery!

The Halo Chain also hosts numerous fundraising efforts, and we plan to grow them even more in 2022! Some events to look out for include a field day, a bake sale, donation bingo, and a 5K!
